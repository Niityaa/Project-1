    Disclipine

    Rules and Regulations for students Be in school on time (9:30 to 9:45) and reach home in time. Attend the morning and evening assembly compulsorily. Be in proper uniform and maintain personal hygiene Maintain your hair as directed by the school. Speak English within the school premises. Do not bring junk food e.g. chewing gum, chocolates, etc., and any food wrappers. Do not visit other classes without the teacher's permission. Do not throw any wastage on the ground. Be in the queue while using toilets, drinking water, etc. Walk normally; do not drag your feet producing sound. Prior information from the guardians is necessary if you have to leave the school during the school hour. Use polite language to speak to your teacher and friends Save the school's property. Do not bring any valuable things like jewels and any other unnecessary things. Any kind of graffiti is strictly prohibited. Inform your teacher if any problem arises. Follow the rules and regulations and make others follow them
